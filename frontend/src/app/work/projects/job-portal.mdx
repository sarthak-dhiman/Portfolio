## Overview

EliteHire is a comprehensive recruitment platform designed to connect top talent with industry leaders. It provides a seamless experience for both job seekers and recruiters, featuring a modern UI and a powerful backend for managing large volumes of data.

## Key Features

- **Advanced Search & Filter**: Find jobs based on location, salary, technology stack, and more.
- **Application Tracking**: Real-time status updates for applicants and a centralized dashboard for recruiters.
- **Resume Management**: Secure storage and retrieval of resumes with automated parsing capabilities.
- **Employer Dashboard**: Tools for posting jobs, managing candidates, and analyzing recruitment metrics.

## Technologies Used

- **Django**
- **React**
- **PostgreSQL**
- **AWS S3 (for Resumes)**
- **Tailwind CSS**

## Impact

The platform successfully streamlined the hiring process for several small-scale organizations during its beta phase, proving its effectiveness in simplifying complex recruitment workflows.
