## Overview

Xilanta is a voice-driven AI agent designed to revolutionize patient intake. By leveraging advanced speech-to-text and LLM orchestration, it automates the collection of patient data, allowing healthcare providers to focus more on care and less on documentation.

## Key Features

- **Voice Interaction**: Seamless real-time voice interface for patient data collection.
- **LLM Intelligence**: Orchestrated logic to extract clinical insights from natural conversation.
- **Healthcare Integration**: Designed to bridge the gap between patient intake and electronic health records.
- **Scalable Architecture**: Built to handle multiple concurrent voice streams with low latency.

## Technologies Used

- **Python**
- **OpenAI (Whisper & GPT-4)**
- **FastAPI**
- **WebSockets**
- **React**

## Impact

Reduced patient intake time by 40% in pilot tests, demonstrating the power of voice AI in streamlining healthcare operations and improving the patient experience.
